WHY SPORTSCONN?

SportsConn’s mission is to connect the world of sport. As the world’s premier sports market network, SportsConn provides a unique, interactive platform for the sports community to engage on a social, professional and commercial platform.


ATHLETES

Whether you’re a pro, amateur or junior athlete, showcase your highlights, upload your stats, and be seen by the global sports community. This is your opportunity to shine.

ORGANIZATIONS

Engage with and target your supporters, customers and athletes in real time and like never before.

SPORTS PROFESSIONALS

Do you work in sport? SportsConn is your platform to connect, recruit and showcase yourself to the global sports market.

FANS

Follow your favourite teams and players; share your favourite pics and videos; connect with friends; and engage with one another on a platform dedicated to sport.
